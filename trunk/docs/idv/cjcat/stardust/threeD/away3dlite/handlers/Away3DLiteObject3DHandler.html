Classpublic class Away3DLiteObject3DHandler
InheritanceAway3DLiteObject3DHandler ParticleHandler StardustElement

This handler adds Object3D particles to a ObjectContainer3D container, removes dead particles from the container, and updates the Object3D objects' x, y, z, rotationX, rotationY, rotationZ, scaleX, scaleY, scaleZ, and alpha properties.
