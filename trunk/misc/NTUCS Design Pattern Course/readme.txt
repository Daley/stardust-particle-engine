This is the written report for my final term project on the Design Pattern course 
offered by the Department of Computer Science of National Taiwan University.
The professor encouraged us to apply Design Patterns in existing projects, instead 
of creating an entire new project solely for this course. And I chose Stardust.
It's written in Chinese, but you can still get the idea from the UML diagrams. And 
the patterns name are written in English, which are not very hard to identify.
